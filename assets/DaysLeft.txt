<h2 aling="center">Backstory</h2>
<hr />
<p>Days Left is a story around the rise of the Legendary Super Saiyan, Jeff Datson. Jeff is on a quest to defeat his twin brother Mark, and put an end to the corrupt Hagerman Industries. Jeff is accompanied by his brother Bill Datson, His Friend Pete, a crazy anime loving scrapyard owner named Earl, and an unknown man who goes by No Name.
